About Us

ASDGC Dental Center was established on February 17, 2020 before the pandemic lockdown was implemented. During its starting period it has provided a quality service to many people around the area but has stopped its operation for more than 3 months when the lock down was implemented.


It offers a wide range quality of services such as Oral Prophylaxis, Dental Extraction, Dentures, Dental Veneers, Braces, Wisdom Tooth Removal, etc to help straighten customer's teeth, to improve and achieve their ideal smile.


ASDGC Dental Center as of now only has 1 branch located in Fortune, Marikina and soon will be establishing another branch in Quezon City. It is owned by a family of doctors and has over a decade of dental practice.
