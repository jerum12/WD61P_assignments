Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide social protection and promote the rights and welfare of the poor, vulnerable and the disadvantaged individuals, family, community and enable them to contribute to poverty alleviation and empowerment, Social Welfare and Development policies, programs, projects and services implemented with or thorough Local Government Unit, Non-Government Organizations, People's Organization, other Government Organizations and other member of civil society

Our vision is to have a society where the poor, vulnerable and disadvantage individuals, families and communities are empowered for an improved quality of life
